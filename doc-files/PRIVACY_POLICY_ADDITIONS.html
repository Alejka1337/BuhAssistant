User-Generated Content Moderation

In Short: We process information related to content reports, user blocking, and moderation to maintain a safe and respectful community environment.

Our Services include community features such as forums and user-generated content. To maintain a safe and respectful environment, we process information in the following ways:

  • Content Reporting. We may process your reports of inappropriate, harmful, or policy-violating content submitted by other users. When you report content (forum posts, comments, or threads), we collect:
    • The type of content being reported (thread or comment)
    • The reason for the report (spam, offensive content, misinformation, inappropriate content, or other)
    • Any additional details you choose to provide
    • The timestamp of when the report was submitted
    This information is used exclusively for content moderation purposes and is reviewed by our moderation team. Reports are kept for record-keeping purposes and to improve our moderation processes.

  • User Blocking. We process information about users you choose to block to enhance your experience and control over the content you see. When you block a user:
    • We store the blocking relationship (your user ID and the blocked user's ID)
    • The blocked user's content (posts, comments, threads) will be hidden from your view
    • The blocked user will not be notified that you have blocked them
    • You can unblock users at any time from your profile settings
    This information is processed solely to filter content and improve your user experience. The blocked user can still access and use the Services normally; they simply will not appear in your feed.

  • Content Moderation. Our moderation team reviews reported content to ensure compliance with our Terms of Service. We may:
    • Review content that has been reported by users
    • Remove content that violates our policies
    • Temporarily or permanently suspend accounts of users who repeatedly violate our terms
    • Maintain logs of moderation actions for transparency and accountability
    All moderation actions are performed by our trained moderation team and comply with applicable laws and our Terms of Service. Users whose content is removed or accounts are suspended will be notified when possible.
